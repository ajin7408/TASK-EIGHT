Creating a Free Trial Azure Cloud Account

Go to Azure Portal:

Visit https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free.
Click on "Start Free":

On the landing page, click on the "Start Free" button.
Sign In with a Microsoft Account:

Use your Microsoft account (Hotmail, Outlook) to sign in. If you don't have one, create a new Microsoft account.
Enter Your Details:

Provide your personal details like name, country, and phone number.
Verify your identity using the code sent to your phone.
Verify Your Identity with a Credit Card:

You'll need to provide a credit card for identity verification. No charges will be made unless you explicitly upgrade to a paid subscription.
Complete the Signup:

Agree to the terms and conditions, and then finish the registration process.
Start Using Azure Free Services:

Once your account is created, you will have access to:
$200 credit for the first 30 days.
Free access to several popular services for 12 months, such as Virtual Machines, Blob Storage, and more.
